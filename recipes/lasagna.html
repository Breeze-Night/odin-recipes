Lasagna

A Spaghetti Cake? I Think Not!

Lasagna is one of the most popular italian dishes ever. It's delicious, simple, and did I mention delicious? By following this recipe, you'll be able to make this dish at home! It's not as dificult as you might think, with the right ingredients and by following the steps, you'll make this dish your go-to to impress anyone!!

Lasagna contains three main components; the pasta itself, protein, and the sauce. We'll break it down on how to prepare the ingredients and eventually... cook it!!

Ingredients

Main Components

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef (around 1.5 kg
  • Mozzarella cheese

Bolognese sauce

  • 3 cans of chopped tomato
  • 250 ml chicken or beef broth
  • 500 ml water
  • salt and pepper
  • an onion diced
  • 2 stalks of celery
  • 170 g canned tomato paste
  • 5 leaves basil
  • parsley

Bechamel Sauce

  • 200 ml whole milk
  • 2-3 tbsp parmesan cheese
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp flour

Steps

How to make bolognese sauce

  1. Sweat the onion and celery inside the pan with low heat for around 5 to 7 minutes.
  2. Increase the heat, add ground beef, tomato paste, chopped tomatoes, broth, water, and basil into the pan. Cook for around 30-40 minutes. Don't forget to stir every 10 minutes.
  3. Add chopped parsley to your liking.
  4. Add salt and pepper according to your taste.

How to make bechamel sauce

  1. Melt the butter and add the flour, mix them well with low heat.
  2. Add milk to the mixture little by little.
  3. Pay attention to the mixture. Try to add more milk if it's too condence, add more flour if the opposite happens.
  4. If the mixture seems consistent, turn off the heat and add parmesan cheese, mix well.

Cook the Lasagna

  1. Boil the lasagna noodle for more or less 5 minutes, then rinse
  2. Take out a baking dish and cover the surface with lasagna sheets. Pour the bolognese sause and the bechamel sauce until the lasagna sheets are covered, then add mozzarella cheese. Layer it again in those exact order for about 4 times minimum.
  3. Set the oven to 180Â°C and put the lasagna inside, wait 20 minutes.
  4. Take out the lasagna and it's time to feast!!
