Lasagna

What is lasagna?

Lasagna is one of the most popular italian dish ever. It's delicious, simple, and did I mention delicious? By following this recipe, you'll be able to make this dish at home! It's not as dificult as you might think, with the right ingredients and by following the steps, you'll make this dish your go-to to impress anyone!!

Lasagna contains three main components; the pasta itself, protein, and the sauce. We'll break it down on how to prepare the ingredients and eventually... cook it!!

Ingredients

Main Components

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef (around 1.5 kg
  • Mozzarella cheese

Bolognese sauce

  • 3 cans of tomato
  • 250 ml chicken or beef broth
  • 500 ml water
  • salt and pepper
  • an onion diced
  • 2 stalks of celery
  • 170 g canned tomato paste
  • 5 leaves basil
  • parsley

Bechamel Sauce

  • 200 ml whole milk
  • 2-3 tbsp parmesan cheese
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp flour
